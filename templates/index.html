REFINE YOUR SEARCH RESULTS

  • SHOE + RED Press ENTER after a keyword to create a deep search.
  • object: CAR Search for objects inside images.
  • cats || dogs OR Search.
  • text: CAR Search for text within images or inside notes.
  • ARTICLES Search by card type (ARTICLES, WEBSITES, NOTES, SNIPPETS).
  • format: PDF Search files by format.
  • YESTERDAY Search by date (YESTERDAY, LAST WEEK, MAY 19TH).
  • SHOE + -RED Exclude something from the search results.
  • "SHOES" To find an exact match (text or text in images).
  • site: YOUTUBE Filter results by a specific website.
  • tag: NAMEOFTAG Filter results by a specific tag.

GLOBAL SHORTCUTS

  • N Shortcut to create a new note.
  • + ENTER Saves note as a new card.
  • ESC Exit a card view.
  • ENTER Focus on the search field.
